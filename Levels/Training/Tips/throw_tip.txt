<tab>Throwing items:<br><br><sp> 1. To execute a quick throw click the 'Throw item' button (G by default), select an item from the list and click the fire button for a throw.<br><sp> 2. To make a series of throws press the 'Grenade' key (5 by default). To select an item to throw, press and hold down the 'Grenade' key until the list appears.<br><tab>You can select an item from the list using arrows or the scroll wheel.<br>
