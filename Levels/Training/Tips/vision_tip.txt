<tab>Enemy's vision:<br><br>- The map marks all of the enemies' fields of vision that may be dangerous for the player.<br>- Fields of vision are divided into two areas. In the grey zone the enemy will not notice you at once. <l="game:Levels\training\tips\threat_tip.txt">The danger scale</l> will warn you. In the colored area the enemy will instantly see you.<br>- Depending on your movement mode, your speed and the enemy's alertness level, his field of vision may change.<br>
