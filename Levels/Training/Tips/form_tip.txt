<tab>Playing the game when dressed in an enemy uniform:<br><br>- When you are wearing an enemy uniform most of your opponents will consider you to be a friendly soldier. In this case they are marked <col=008000>green</col> on the map. <br>- If you do not take <l="game:Levels\training\tips\dc_tip.txt">suspicious actions</l> - your opponents will not react to you. <br> - <col=D20000>Red</col> is used to show the enemies, who can recognize you from a short distance. They usually have a higher rank than you, or patrol the area.<br><tab>Try to avoid encountering them.<br>
